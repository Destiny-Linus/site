What is a Website?

A website is your business's online home

It's the central place where people can learn about:

  • Who you are
  • What you offer
  • How to contact or trust you

Unlike social media, a website is fully yours, you control the design, message and experience.

What a Website Does for Your Business

A well-built website helps you:

  • Look professional and credible.
  • Explain your services clearly.
  • Be found on Google.
  • Build trust before customers contact you.

It works as your online headquarters, supporting everything else you do such as ads, social media, WhatsApp, and referrals.
